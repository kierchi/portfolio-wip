Teeny Me

A creative dress-up game where you design your avatar, style outfits, and build your dream space

The Concept

Teeny Me is a mobile dress-up game that brings back the joy of childhood creativity. It started as a school UI design project, but grew into something I'm passionate about turning into a real game.

I drew inspiration from the dress-up games I loved as a kid—sites like Dressupgames.com and Doll Divine. As an artist, I wanted to create something authentic by drawing all the game assets myself, giving it a personal, hand-crafted feel.

I've built a rough playable demo that shows the core mechanics, and I'm excited about the potential to develop this further.

Game Preview

Create your own unique avatar, mix and match endless outfit combinations, design your dream room, and take care of your virtual character in this creative and expressive experience.

Design Process

Concept Art

Early character designs exploring different styles, expressions, and outfit possibilities. Each character was hand-drawn to capture a unique personality and aesthetic.

Wireframes & Layout Design

Exploring the app's information architecture and user flow through low-fidelity wireframes.

Figma Prototype

Interactive prototype showcasing the final UI design, navigation flow, and user interactions.

Key Features

Create Your Avatar

Design a unique character with customizable features, expressions, and accessories

Mix & Match Outfits

Endless clothing combinations to express your personal style

Design Your Room

Decorate and customize your avatar's living space with furniture and decor

Take Care of Your Avatar

Feed, play with, and nurture your character to keep them happy

The Vision

Teeny Me started as a class project, but it's grown into something I'm passionate about developing into a full mobile game. The positive feedback on the concept and art style has motivated me to continue building it out.

I'm currently working on expanding the asset library, refining the gameplay mechanics, and improving the overall user experience. My goal is to create a game that captures the creativity and joy of classic dress-up games while bringing something fresh and personal to the genre.

If you're interested in collaborating or learning more about the project, I'd love to hear from you!
