Teeny Me

What is it?

A Dress Up Game

Teeny Me is a dress up game for mobile devices. It originally started off a school project where we had to design a UI.

I drew inspiration from my childhood, going on the Dressupgames.com or Doll Divine. And since I consider myself an artist I wanted to draw the assets myself.

I've made a "playable" demo although it's quite rough around the edges.
