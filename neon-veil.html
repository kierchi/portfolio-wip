NEON VEIL

What is it?

Brand Identity for an Indie Music Agency

Neon Veil is a music agency built to amplify indie and alternative artists — the ones who don't get handed a spotlight. The brand is raw, unconventional, and unapologetically loud. This is the full identity package: designed to match the energy of the artists it champions

The Origin

The whole palette came from one image. There's something in that murky teal — the grain, the low light — that felt exactly like the space Neon Veil lives in. Underground, but electric.
