Other Bio

I’m a graphic designer and illustrator who just really loves making things. I’ve always been drawn to creative work — from photography to sketching — and eventually found my place in graphic design, where I can combine structure with creativity.

I work under the name Gold in Winter, inspired by my love for winter, colour, and a subtle connection to my Chinese roots. My style leans into being cute, colourful, and simple, and I aim to create work that people genuinely enjoy looking at and using. Right now, I’m focused on growing my skills, building my portfolio, and moving toward freelance work.
