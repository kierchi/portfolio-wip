Hello, I'm Kaila Wong

A Graphic Designer from Vancouver!

I originally dreamed of becoming a photographer, then an animator, and somewhere along the way I realized what I was really drawn to was the process of creating — capturing moments, building narratives, and bringing ideas to life visually.

That curiosity led me to the New Media Design and Web Development program at BCIT, where I developed a strong interest in graphic design and video editing.

I'm someone who notices the small things like the way layouts guide the eye, how motion adds meaning, how documentation preserves memory.

Whether it's branding, digital content, or motion graphics, I enjoy crafting work that feels intentional and human.

What I do:

  • Social Media Graphics
  • Layout & Print Design
  • Video Editing
  • Motion Graphics
